Prevention Coronavirus

The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed to this virus.

Wash your hands often with soap

Wash your hands atleast for 20 seconds after you have been in a public place.

Avoid touching your eyes, nose, and mouth

Try not to touch eyes, nose and mouth, etc. without washing hands.

Avoid close contact with people who are sick

Fully avoid any visit or contact with any person having symptoms

Stay home if you are sick, get medical care

If you are sick, report to the administration, get medical care and stay home

Cover your mouth and nose with mask

Always use a face mask while stepping out of the house.

Immediately wash your hands with water

After getting into the house wash your hands with soap immediately

If you are sick: You should wear a facemask

If you are sick use facemask all the time for precautive measures.

If surfaces are dirty, clean them

Keep your house sanitized and clean all the time to avoid any disease
